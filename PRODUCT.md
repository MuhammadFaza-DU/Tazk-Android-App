# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Tazk is for Gen Z users, especially students and young workers, who manage daily tasks, habits, study/work routines, and personal productivity from an Android phone. They use it in short daily sessions: checking what matters today, completing tasks/habits, starting focus sessions, and tracking progress without needing an account or internet connection.

## Product Purpose

Tazk combines task management, habit tracking, Pomodoro focus, local reminders, homescreen widgets, and lightweight RPG-style gamification in one offline Android app. Success means users can structure their day, keep streaks alive, feel progress through XP/levels/badges, and complete common workflows quickly from either the app or homescreen widgets.

## Brand Personality

Playful, growth-oriented, and encouraging. The product should feel warm, colorful, and motivating without becoming childish or visually noisy. The tone should make daily productivity feel lighter and more rewarding while staying useful and structured.

## Anti-references

Avoid cold enterprise productivity UI, generic monochrome task lists, aggressive dopamine-heavy game UI, and childish cartoon styling. Avoid visuals that make the app feel like a marketing landing page instead of a working daily tool. Me+ is a useful reference for warm, structured self-care/productivity, but Tazk should keep its own growth/RPG identity.

## Design Principles

- Make today's next action obvious.
- Reward progress without distracting from the workflow.
- Keep offline/local behavior trustworthy and visible.
- Use playful feedback for completion, streaks, XP, and unlocks.
- Preserve calm structure even when the palette is colorful.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for readable mobile contrast, clear tap targets, text that does not overflow on small Android screens, and motion that feels gentle rather than overwhelming. Effects should provide feedback, but core task/habit completion must remain understandable without relying only on animation or color.
